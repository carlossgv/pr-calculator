## Current state
- The percent picker is now an inline detail plus a bounded scroll region.
- The user reported that this still feels off, specifically because the interaction should be more like a contained spinner and less like a nested scrolling page.
- The rest of the UX cleanup is stable and committed in `d5bf075`.

## Decisions made
- Keep the app mobile-first and single-column where it improves scanning and touch use.
- Prefer inline detail over a modal for the percent picker.
- Treat the percent picker as a bounded control area rather than a full-page list.

## Next steps
1. Rework the percent picker into a clearer spinner-style control, likely with a tighter visible window and stronger selection affordance.
2. Evaluate whether the picker should scroll vertically only inside a fixed-height viewport, or whether the cards should become a more explicit wheel/list hybrid.
3. Re-run `tsc` and `vitest` after the next UI change.

## Blockers / open questions
- The exact spinner behavior is still not settled.
- Need a decision on whether the picker should preserve the two-column layout on desktop or switch to a single-column wheel everywhere.
