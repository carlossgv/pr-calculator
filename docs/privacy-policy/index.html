Privacy Policy

PR Calc

Effective date: February 5, 2026

Summary

PR Calc is a workout and PR tracking app. We do not sell your data. We only collect the minimum information needed to keep the app working and, if enabled, to synchronize your data across devices.

Data We Collect

  • Workout data you create in the app (movements, PR entries, and preferences).
  • A device identifier and device token used to authorize sync.

How We Use Data

  • To store your workout data locally on your device.
  • To synchronize your data with our server when sync is enabled.
  • To provide basic app functionality (e.g., backup and restore).

What We Do Not Collect

  • No names, emails, or phone numbers are required to use the app.
  • No payment information is collected by the app.
  • No advertising identifiers or analytics profiles are used.

Third-Party Services

The app includes an optional external link to Ko-fi for donations. If you choose to use it, Ko-fi handles any payment information directly under their own policies.

Data Retention

Data is stored locally on your device. If sync is enabled, data is stored on our servers to keep your devices in sync. You can remove local data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at contact@carlosgv.dev.
